Gadgets

The main accessories to enjoy the maximum of the Playstation VR.

PlayStation VR aim controller

Bring precision aim to compatible PS VR shooters with this advanced controller designed specifically for PlayStation VR. PlayStation Move

Built to connect you intuitively with virtual worlds, PlayStation Move motion controllers allow you to effortlessly interact with your virtual reality environment, mimicking real-world actions for an incredible sense of presence in compatible PlayStation VR games.

Plantronics RIG 4VR

Designed to naturally compliment your PS VR headset, the RIG4VR officially-licenced headphones have an ultra-lightweight construction to keep you immersed for longer and a detachable 0.4m cable for enhanced freedom of movement and optimum comfort.

PlayStation Camera

The incredibly accurate one-to-one tracking of PlayStation VR is made possible by the PlayStation camera. Depth-sensing dual lenses track every movement of your PS VR headset, DUALSHOCK 4 or PlayStation Move Controllers replicating those actions in virtual reality for incredible immersion.
